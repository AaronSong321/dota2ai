Ranked Matchmaking AI v1.1

Hello, everyone, I am adamqqq.Welcome to play with our ai, the script aims to achieve a high level of man-machine confrontation. Our ultimate goal is to simulate the Ranked Matchmaking gaming experience (ha ha ha). The script partly take over bot hero, the rest implement by the default program. Players can be in any position, the default support 5v5 game.
